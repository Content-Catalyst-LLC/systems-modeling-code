# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- The system can be represented by one stock.
- Reinforcing inflow is constrained by carrying capacity.
- Corrective outflow depends on delayed stock.
- Threshold pressure activates above a defined stock level.
- Shock response is represented as a discrete disturbance.
- Scenarios reveal structural differences.

## Limitations

- The model is not empirically calibrated.
- The stock is abstract.
- Real systems may require multiple stocks, sectors, agents, networks, delays, events, and spatial structure.
- Validation checks are synthetic plausibility checks.
- Outputs should not be interpreted as forecasts.
