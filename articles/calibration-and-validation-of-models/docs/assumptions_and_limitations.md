# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- Observed data are synthetic.
- The data-generating process is logistic-style growth with noise.
- Calibration estimates growth rate and carrying capacity.
- The validation period is held out from calibration.
- Error metrics are used as validation diagnostics.
- Benchmarks are included for comparison.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is represented.
- No structural uncertainty is fully simulated.
- No Bayesian calibration is included.
- No formal identifiability analysis is included.
- Results should not be interpreted as real-world findings.
