# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Trust and uptake are represented on a 0 to 1 scale.
- Policy intensity adjusts adaptively based on a target-state gap.
- Institutional capacity adjusts with delay.
- Administrative burden and side effects accumulate with policy intensity.
- Scenarios are illustrative and not calibrated.

## Limitations

- No real policy, agency, jurisdiction, program, statute, community, or implementation system is represented.
- Political behavior, legal interpretation, culture, rights, power, and legitimacy are simplified.
- Equity dimensions are conceptual rather than empirical.
- Applied use requires domain evidence, implementation review, calibration, validation, uncertainty analysis, and public accountability.
