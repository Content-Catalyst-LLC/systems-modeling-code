# Robustness Protocol

Robustness analysis asks whether strategies remain acceptable across many uncertain and stressful futures.

## Metrics included

- Mean resilience score
- Lower-tail resilience score
- Worst-case resilience score
- Minimum service ratio
- Failure share
- Mean regret
- Maximum regret
- Recovery time
- Cumulative unmet demand

## Professional extension

Applied robustness analysis should use domain-specific thresholds, model comparison, stakeholder review, and explicit uncertainty-space documentation.
