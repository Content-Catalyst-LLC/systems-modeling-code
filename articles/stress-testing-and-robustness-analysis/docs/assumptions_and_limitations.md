# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Service capacity is simplified.
- Stress futures are randomly generated from illustrative ranges.
- Policies are simplified redundancy and adaptive-response strategies.
- Failure threshold is represented by service ratio below 0.85.
- Regret compares strategies within each synthetic future.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is represented.
- No real-world infrastructure, health, climate, or financial system is calibrated.
- No formal probability distribution is validated.
- No structural model ensemble is fully implemented.
- Results should not be used as real-world decision guidance.
