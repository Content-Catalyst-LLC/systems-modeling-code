# Stress Testing Protocol

Stress testing evaluates performance under adverse, extreme, boundary, or failure-oriented conditions.

## Core questions

- What stressors are imposed?
- How severe are the stressors?
- How long do they last?
- Do stressors occur alone or in combination?
- What counts as failure?
- Does the system recover?
- Which strategy reduces unacceptable outcomes?

## Companion workflow

The companion workflows test dynamic service capacity under demand growth, capacity loss, shock duration, recovery drag, redundancy, and adaptive response.
