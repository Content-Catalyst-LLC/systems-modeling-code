# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- Infrastructure is represented as a small static graph.
- Nodes are either functional or failed.
- Dependencies are known.
- Load redistribution is simplified.
- Overload thresholds are deterministic.
- Criticality weights are synthetic.
- Recovery timing is simplified.

## Limitations

- No geospatial hazard layer.
- No detailed engineering physics.
- No hydraulic, power-flow, traffic-assignment, or telecom-routing model.
- No social vulnerability layer.
- No real outage calibration.
- No repair crew routing or logistics.
- No institutional coordination model.

This is a learning model, not an operational decision model.
