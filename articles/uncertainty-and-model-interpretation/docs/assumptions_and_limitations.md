# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Scenarios are sampled from illustrative ranges.
- Policies are simple control and adaptation rules.
- Robustness is measured through lower-tail resilience, regret, and worst-case behavior.
- Confidence notes are rule-based summaries, not expert judgments.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is represented.
- No formal probability model is validated.
- No stakeholder value process is included.
- No structural ensemble beyond documented variants is implemented.
- Results should not be used as real-world decision guidance.
