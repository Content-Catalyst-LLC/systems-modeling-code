# Diagnostics Notes

Clarification and distortion diagnostics are interpretive safeguards. They do not prove that a model is valid. They help identify whether a model is likely to improve understanding or mislead users in a particular use context.

## Useful diagnostics

- Clarification score
- Distortion risk score
- Net interpretive value
- Valid-use label
- False precision risk
- Boundary risk
- Proxy risk
- Misuse risk
- Communication controls
- Scope-of-use register
- Risk register

The goal is responsible model literacy: understand what the model helps users see and what it makes easier to overlook.
