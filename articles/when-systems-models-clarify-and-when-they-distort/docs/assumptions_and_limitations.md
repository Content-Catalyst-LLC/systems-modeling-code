# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Scores are normalized from zero to one.
- Clarification is represented through structural, dynamic, scenario, and assumption-transparency dimensions.
- Distortion is represented through false precision, boundary, proxy, and misuse risks.
- Net interpretive value is clarification score minus distortion risk score.
- Use labels are illustrative.

## Limitations

- The workflow does not validate real models.
- The scoring system is a review scaffold, not a decision rule.
- Applied use requires domain validation, stakeholder review, uncertainty analysis, documentation, and governance.
