# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Feedback structures are intentionally simplified.
- Delay effects are modeled as discrete lagged recurrences.
- Stock-flow dynamics use simplified inflow and outflow rules.
- Policy-resistance scenarios are illustrative.
- Loop-dominance labels are diagnostic and not empirical claims.

## Limitations

- No real-world system is calibrated.
- No empirical causal evidence is provided.
- No formal structural validation is performed.
- Policy results are not decision guidance.
- Applied use requires domain data, validation, stakeholder review, and uncertainty analysis.
