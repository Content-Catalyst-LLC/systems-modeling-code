# Responsible Use

AI-enhanced systems models can influence infrastructure investment, public health response, environmental regulation, climate planning, social services, workforce decisions, finance, education, transportation, emergency management, and other high-stakes decisions.

Do not use these synthetic workflows for real decisions without:

- Domain validation
- Data governance
- Privacy review
- Bias and subgroup performance testing
- Drift monitoring
- Human oversight
- Contestability and appeal mechanisms where people are affected
- Clear model documentation
- Security and misuse review
- Responsible communication of uncertainty and limits

AI should strengthen systems reasoning, not replace accountability.
