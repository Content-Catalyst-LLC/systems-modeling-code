# Stock-Flow Modeling Protocol

Stock-flow modeling translates accumulated conditions into explicit stocks, inflows, outflows, feedback relationships, and net-flow diagnostics.

## Core questions

- What persistent condition is accumulating?
- What is the stock unit?
- What is the time step?
- What inflows add to the stock?
- What outflows subtract from the stock?
- Are units dimensionally consistent?
- Does the stock influence its own future flows?
- Are there delays between stock pressure and corrective flow?
- Are flows linear, nonlinear, capacity-constrained, or threshold-based?
- What scenarios reverse, stabilize, or worsen the stock trajectory?

## Companion workflow

The companion workflows simulate backlog accumulation, resource depletion and regeneration, infrastructure condition, delayed response, high-demand stress, and adaptive recovery.
