# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Backlog arrivals and completions are stylized.
- Resource regeneration follows a simple logistic form.
- Infrastructure condition is represented as a bounded index from 0 to 100.
- Maintenance increases condition while wear decreases condition.
- Policy timing is represented through scenario switches.
- Recovery is defined by stock-specific thresholds for demonstration.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is calibrated.
- No real infrastructure, resource, health, or service system is represented.
- Social and institutional stock examples are conceptual.
- No spatial variation is modeled.
- Applied use requires domain data, structural validation, uncertainty analysis, and stakeholder review.
