# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Stakeholder groups are simplified categories.
- Outcome weights are normalized.
- Scenario values are normalized from zero to one.
- Disagreement is represented through score dispersion.
- The legitimacy-adjusted score penalizes disagreement.
- Assumption registers are illustrative.

## Limitations

- This workflow does not replace real facilitation.
- It does not conduct stakeholder engagement, consent, power analysis, or participatory governance.
- It does not capture qualitative disagreement, historical injustice, political authority, local knowledge, or lived experience in full.
- Applied use requires inclusive process design, documentation of dissent, community review, ethical data governance, and accountability for model use.
