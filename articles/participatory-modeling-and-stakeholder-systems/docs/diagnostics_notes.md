# Participatory Modeling Diagnostics Notes

The central diagnostic is not whether the group reaches agreement. The central diagnostic is whether the model makes assumptions, values, disagreement, boundaries, and missing voices visible enough to support responsible interpretation.

## Useful diagnostics

- Stakeholder weight sums
- Stakeholder-specific scenario scores
- Mean scenario score
- Disagreement standard deviation
- Minimum and maximum stakeholder score
- Legitimacy-adjusted score
- Assumption status counts
- Contested assumption count
- Missing stakeholder review
- Participation-level review
- Facilitation risk review

A participatory model should be evaluated both technically and procedurally.
