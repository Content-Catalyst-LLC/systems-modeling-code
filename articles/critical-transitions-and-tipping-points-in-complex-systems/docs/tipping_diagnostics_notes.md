# Tipping Diagnostics Notes

Critical-transition diagnostics should be interpreted as evidence, not deterministic prophecy.

## Useful diagnostics

- Approximate transition step
- Forward and backward transition locations
- Estimated hysteresis gap
- Minimum and maximum state
- Rolling variance
- Lag-1 autocorrelation
- Path dependence between forward and backward forcing
- Scenario comparison under different forcing rates
- Model sensitivity to integration time step

Early-warning signals can produce false positives and false negatives. They should be combined with structural modeling, domain evidence, and uncertainty analysis.
