# Responsible Use

Delay and policy-resistance models can improve policy humility. They can also be misused to dismiss legitimate opposition as irrational resistance.

Do not use these synthetic workflows for real decisions without:

- Domain evidence
- Boundary review
- Delay sensitivity analysis
- Structural validation
- Stakeholder review
- Distributional impact analysis
- Legitimacy and fairness review
- Clear communication of uncertainty and model limits

Policy resistance should be used to improve design and accountability, not to blame affected communities or justify coercive escalation.
