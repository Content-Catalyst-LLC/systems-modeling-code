# Validation Protocol

For this synthetic companion model, validation checks workflow integrity and plausible numerical behavior.

## Checks

- Delay taxonomy is exported.
- Policy-resistance examples are exported.
- Scenario trajectories are generated.
- Summary diagnostics are generated.
- State remains nonnegative and finite.
- Target crossings remain bounded.
- Overshoot and mean target gap remain nonnegative.
- Cumulative intervention and counterresponse remain nonnegative.
- Resistance ratio remains bounded.

## Professional extension

Applied use should add empirical validation, behavior reproduction, delay estimation, policy scenario review, sensitivity analysis, uncertainty propagation, model comparison, and distributional impact analysis.
