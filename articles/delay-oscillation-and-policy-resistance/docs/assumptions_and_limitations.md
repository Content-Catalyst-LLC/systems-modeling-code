# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Lower state values represent reduced modeled pressure.
- Corrective intervention responds to perceived target gap.
- Perceived state is smoothed from actual state.
- Counterresponse is proportional to intervention effort.
- Delay is represented as a fixed discrete lag.
- Target crossings are an illustrative oscillation diagnostic.

## Limitations

- No empirical public policy, supply chain, infrastructure, climate, health, or organizational system is calibrated.
- Human behavior is simplified.
- Institutional legitimacy and political feasibility are not modeled directly.
- Delays are scenario assumptions rather than estimated values.
- Applied use requires domain data, structural validation, uncertainty analysis, and stakeholder review.
