# Delay, Oscillation, and Policy Resistance Modeling Protocol

Delay-aware systems modeling treats timing, feedback, perception, intervention, and counterresponse as part of the system structure.

## Core questions

- What stock or state is policy trying to move toward a target?
- What is the actual state?
- What is the perceived state?
- How long does information take to arrive?
- How long does decision and implementation take?
- How strongly does the policy respond to the perceived gap?
- Does the system counterrespond to the intervention?
- Does the response create overshoot, oscillation, or resistance?
- Which delay assumptions drive the result?

## Companion workflow

The companion workflows compare timely correction, delayed correction, overcorrection, undercorrection, policy resistance, slow recognition, fast overreaction, and adaptive moderated response.
