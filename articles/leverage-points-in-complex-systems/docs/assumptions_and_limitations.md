# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Lower system state means lower modeled burden.
- Feedback gain represents the system's tendency to reproduce pressure.
- Information delay and information quality influence how corrective action responds.
- Rules can change feedback strength once stress crosses a threshold.
- Goal shifts increase resilience investment.
- Self-organization increases adaptive learning capacity.
- Leverage ratio is illustrative, not a universal measure.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is calibrated.
- No real-world policy recommendation is made.
- No formal institutional feasibility analysis is included.
- No stakeholder legitimacy assessment is included.
- Applied use requires domain data, structural validation, uncertainty analysis, and governance review.
