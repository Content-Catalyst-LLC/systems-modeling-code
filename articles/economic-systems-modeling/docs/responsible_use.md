# Responsible Use

Economic systems models can influence policy, budgets, livelihoods, financial regulation, climate strategy, labor markets, housing, and public investment. They should be used with humility and transparency.

Do not use these synthetic workflows for real decisions without:

- Domain evidence
- Data audit
- Model calibration
- Validation against historical behavior
- Accounting consistency checks
- Uncertainty and sensitivity analysis
- Distributional impact assessment
- Stakeholder review
- Clear communication of limitations

Economic models are structured arguments, not automatic truth machines. They should make mechanisms and tradeoffs visible.
