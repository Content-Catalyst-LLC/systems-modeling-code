# Validation Protocol

For this synthetic companion model, validation checks workflow integrity and plausible numerical behavior.

## Checks

- Economic component taxonomy is exported.
- Feedback-loop taxonomy is exported.
- Modeling approaches and sectoral balance examples are exported.
- Scenario trajectories are generated.
- Summary diagnostics are generated.
- Output, capital, debt, consumption, investment, debt service, and fragility remain nonnegative.
- Demand gap remains finite.
- Validation checks are exported.
