# Health Systems Diagnostics Notes

Health diagnostics should track effective capacity, unmet need, workforce strain, access, trust, and equity.

## Useful diagnostics

- Care demand trajectory
- Effective capacity trajectory
- Demand-capacity pressure
- Backlog trajectory
- Burnout trajectory
- Attrition trajectory
- Served volume
- Unmet need
- Access gap
- Trust trajectory
- Scenario comparison
- Validation checks

The key modeling question is not only how many services are delivered, but whether health need is being met safely, equitably, sustainably, and with public trust.
