# Calibration Notes

Calibration aligns a model with observed evidence when the modeling purpose requires empirical fit.

## Calibration inputs

- Initial conditions
- Time-series observations
- Network structure
- Flow rates
- Shock histories
- Recovery rates
- Behavioral parameters
- External drivers

## Calibration cautions

A model can fit historical data for the wrong structural reason. Historical fit does not guarantee future validity, especially under structural change, policy change, regime shifts, or altered incentives.
