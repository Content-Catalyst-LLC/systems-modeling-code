# Validation Protocol

For this synthetic companion model, validation checks workflow integrity and plausible numerical behavior.

## Checks

- Cascade mechanism taxonomy is exported.
- Domain examples and containment strategies are exported.
- Scenario trajectories are generated.
- Summary diagnostics are generated.
- Affected share remains between zero and one.
- Affected count and new failures remain nonnegative.
- Cascade duration remains nonnegative.
- Degree statistics remain nonnegative.
- Validation checks are exported.
