# Cascade Diagnostics Notes

Cascade diagnostics should distinguish exposure from failure and correlation from contagion.

## Useful diagnostics

- Final affected share
- Final affected count
- Cascade duration
- Maximum new failures in a step
- Mean degree
- Maximum degree
- Scenario comparison across thresholds and connectivity
- Containment labels
- Propagation mechanism taxonomy
- Domain cascade examples

The most important question is not whether a cascade can be produced, but whether the modeled mechanism is plausible for the system being studied.
