# Responsible Use

Digital twins can influence infrastructure maintenance, industrial operations, urban planning, energy systems, environmental management, health-system operations, logistics, aerospace engineering, emergency response, and public services. Their outputs may shape real-world action.

Do not use these synthetic workflows for real decisions without:

- Domain engineering review
- Data governance
- Sensor validation
- Cybersecurity review
- Privacy assessment
- Model calibration and validation
- Drift monitoring
- Human oversight
- Escalation and override rules
- Audit trails and accountability
- Responsible communication of uncertainty and limits

Digital twins should improve understanding, monitoring, and decision support. They should not obscure uncertainty, automate accountability away, or create false confidence through polished interfaces.
