# Responsible Use

Panarchy can clarify resilience, disturbance, renewal, and transformation across scales. It can also be misused to naturalize collapse or excuse preventable harm.

Do not use these synthetic workflows for real decisions without:

- Domain evidence
- Boundary review
- Scale-boundary justification
- Stakeholder review
- Distributional impact analysis
- Scenario uncertainty
- Structural validation
- Ethical assessment of what should persist or transform

Panarchy should deepen accountability, not describe crisis as a neutral or inevitable natural cycle.
