# Complexity Mathematics Protocol

A professional complexity-math workflow should answer:

1. What is the system question?
2. What state variables are required?
3. What interaction structure matters?
4. Is the process continuous, discrete, stochastic, networked, spatial, or agent-based?
5. Which mathematical representation fits the mechanism?
6. Which assumptions are empirical, theoretical, or illustrative?
7. Which outputs are interpretable?
8. Which uncertainty and robustness checks are required?
9. What cannot be inferred from the model?

Mathematics should clarify assumptions, not conceal them.
