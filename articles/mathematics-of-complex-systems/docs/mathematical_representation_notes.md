# Mathematical Representation Notes

The companion workflows use simplified mathematical examples to demonstrate core complex systems concepts.

## Included representations

- Logistic-map recurrence
- Sensitivity to initial conditions
- Bifurcation sampling
- Synthetic graph construction
- Adjacency and degree analysis
- Network diffusion
- Stochastic shocks
- Entropy and dispersion diagnostics

## Professional extension

Applied systems modeling should connect mathematical form to domain evidence, boundary judgment, calibration, validation, uncertainty analysis, and decision context.
