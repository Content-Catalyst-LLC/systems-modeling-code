# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- Observed data are synthetic.
- Model families are deliberately simple.
- Calibration and validation periods are split by time.
- Ensemble weighting uses inverse validation RMSE.
- Policy comparison is illustrative and synthetic.
- Model-dependence notes are documented but not fully quantified.

## Limitations

- No empirical system is represented.
- No formal Bayesian model averaging is implemented.
- No real-world multi-model ensemble is included.
- Policy scores are synthetic.
- Results should not be treated as decision guidance.
