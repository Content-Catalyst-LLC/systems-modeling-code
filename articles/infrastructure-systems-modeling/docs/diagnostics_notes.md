# Infrastructure Systems Diagnostics Notes

Infrastructure diagnostics should track service, not only asset status.

## Useful diagnostics

- Sector service availability
- Composite service availability
- Unmet service share
- Cumulative unmet service
- Minimum service by sector
- Recovery trajectory
- Shock duration
- Interdependency strength
- Demand-capacity gap
- Critical dependency pathways
- Validation checks

The key modeling question is not only whether a component failed, but whether critical services remain available, who loses service, and how disruption propagates through dependent systems.
