# Ethics, Power, and Systems Modeling Protocol

Ethical model review should be part of model construction, not a final paragraph added after outputs are produced.

Core questions:

- Who defines the model purpose?
- Who sets the system boundary?
- Who provides data and who is missing from the data?
- Which stakeholders are affected, represented, influential, or excluded?
- Which groups face high burden but low influence?
- Which assumptions, proxies, and objectives embed values?
- How is uncertainty communicated?
- What uses of the model are valid or invalid?
- Who can challenge or appeal model outputs?
- Who remains accountable when the model causes harm?
