# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- All data are synthetic.
- Workload, capacity, learning, burnout, attrition, trust, and backlog are simplified.
- Trust is represented on a 0 to 1 scale.
- Capacity is treated as a composite index, not literal headcount.
- Scenarios are illustrative and not calibrated.
- Equity, culture, and power dimensions are represented conceptually.

## Limitations

- No real organization, team, workforce, culture, or management system is represented.
- Human behavior is simplified.
- Capacity is not interchangeable across roles, skills, relationships, and contexts.
- Applied use requires privacy review, consent, stakeholder validation, organizational evidence, calibration, uncertainty analysis, and ethical governance.
