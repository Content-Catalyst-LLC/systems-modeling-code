# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- The system can be represented as one stock.
- Reinforcing inflow is limited by carrying capacity.
- Balancing response depends on delayed stock.
- Threshold correction activates above a defined level.
- A one-time shock can be introduced.
- Scenario comparison can reveal structural behavior.

## Limitations

- No empirical data are used.
- The stock is abstract.
- Real systems may require multiple stocks, agents, networks, events, sectors, and spatial structure.
- Validation checks are synthetic plausibility checks.
- Outputs should not be interpreted as real-world findings.
