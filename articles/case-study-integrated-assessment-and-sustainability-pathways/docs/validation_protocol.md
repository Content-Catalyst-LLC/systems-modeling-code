# Validation Protocol

Synthetic validation checks:

- Pathway runs are generated.
- Clean energy share remains between zero and one.
- Adaptation capacity remains between zero and one.
- Equity score remains between zero and one.
- Annual emissions remain nonnegative.
- Land and water indicators remain between zero and one.
- Pathway summaries are generated.

Applied validation would require historical data, domain-specific model comparison, scenario review, stakeholder review, sensitivity analysis, and uncertainty ensembles.
