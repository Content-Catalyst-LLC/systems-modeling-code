# Validation Protocol

Synthetic validation checks:

- Scenario runs are generated.
- Service level remains between zero and one.
- Adaptive capacity remains between zero and one.
- Degradation remains between zero and one.
- Climate stress remains nonnegative.
- Scenario summaries are generated.

Applied validation would require local climate hazards, observed disruption records, infrastructure or ecosystem performance data, recovery histories, social vulnerability indicators, and stakeholder-defined thresholds.
