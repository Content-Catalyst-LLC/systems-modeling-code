# Scenario Design Notes

This companion folder uses synthetic scenario definitions to demonstrate scenario modeling workflow structure.

## Scenario categories

- Baseline
- Policy intervention
- Stress shock
- Exploratory rapid growth
- Resilience investment
- Compound stress

## Design logic

Scenarios vary:

- Growth pressure
- Policy drag
- Shock timing
- Shock severity
- Resilience investment
- Policy strategy

## Professional extension

Applied scenario modeling should document why scenarios are plausible, relevant, diverse, internally coherent, and useful for the decision or research question.
